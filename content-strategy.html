Value Proposition

As a content strategist, I align documentation goals with business objectives, employing a user-centered approach to enhance product adoption and usability. My work focuses on developing scalable content strategies, ensuring consistent messaging, and establishing best practices for content operations.

  • Strategic planning: Lead content strategy for large-scale projects, defining key messaging, style guides, and taxonomy frameworks to ensure a unified voice.
  • Persona-based documentation: Develop content tailored to various user personas, resulting in improved engagement and reduced support requests.
  • Content management: Establish workflows for content creation, review, and publication to maintain quality and consistency.

Tools & Technologies

I utilize a suite of tools to streamline content strategy processes, including:

  • Content Management Systems (CMS): WordPress, Drupal
  • Collaboration Platforms: Confluence, Jira, SharePoint
  • Content Design & Planning: Figma, Miro
  • Analytics & Reporting: Google Analytics, Hotjar
