Value Proposition

With over 13 years of experience in technical writing, I specialize in creating clear, accessible documentation that empowers users, developers, and stakeholders to make the most out of technology. My expertise lies in crafting user-centric content, including:

  • Overviews and Conceptual Guides: Developed comprehensive overviews for complex AI and IoT solutions, breaking down intricate technical details into easy-to-understand content for diverse audiences.
  • User Guides: Delivered step-by-step guides for various software products, providing users with intuitive instructions on installation, configuration, and use cases.
  • Get Started Guides: Created quick start guides that help users and developers onboard efficiently, reducing learning curves and accelerating product adoption.
  • API Documentation: Authored in-depth API references, endpoint documentation, and integration guides, enabling developers to seamlessly integrate platform capabilities into their applications.
  • Developer Portals and Microsites: Collaborated with UX architects to design and populate developer portals with targeted content, increasing developer satisfaction and reducing support tickets by 15%.
