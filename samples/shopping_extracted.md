Getting started 
You can buy your favorite sporting gear and accessories from the online shopping portal. Our online 
shopping portal features the latest products, discounts, rewards, and more. 
This guide will introduce you to the online shopping portal and its features. In this section, you can find 
information for the most common tasks that you will perform while shopping. To learn more about any 
specific scenario, you can find information in the following sections: 
Log in  
You should log in to the shopping portal to be able to use all the available features. As a prerequisite, 
you must have an account to login to the shopping portal. 
If you are a new customer or using the shopping portal for the first time then you can first sign-up to 
register. To learn more, see Sign up.  
To log in: 
1. In the internet browser, enter https://www.decathlon.in. 
2. On the home page, click Sign in. 
3. In the LOGIN dialog box, in the Enter your email/phone number field, enter the email or phone 
number. 
4. Click LOGIN VIA OTP. 
You will be logged in to the shopping portal. 
 
 
 
Buying on the shopping portal 
The shopping portal is designed to provide you a hassle-free shopping experience with the convenience 
to buy what you need without stepping out of your home. This section outlines the tasks that you need 
to perform to buy products and accessories on the shopping portal. 
Create an account 
You can skip this step, if you already have an account registered and proceed with the next task. 
You will require an account to log in to the shopping portal and complete your order. An account will 
also contain your details and you can view or manage your orders from your account on the shopping 
portal. To create an account, you should sign-up or register on the shopping portal. 
To create an account: 
1. On the home page, click Sign in. 
2. In the LOGIN dialog box, click the SIGNUP tab. 
3. In the Enter your email/phone number field, enter the email or phone number. 
4. Click CONTINUE. 
5. Enter the OTP (4 digits). 
6. Click VALIDATE OTP. 
Your account will be created on the shopping portal. 
Update profile information 
After creating your account, you can update your profile to add your details such as name, mobile 
number, email address, and so on.  
To update personal information: 
1. Log in to the shopping portal. 
2. On the home page, click Account. 
3. Click My profile.  
4. On the My Profile tab, in the Personal Information section, enter the required details. 
5. Click Update. 
To update your address: 
1. If required, log in to the shopping portal. 
2. On the home page, click Account. 
3. Click My profile.  
4. Click the Address tab, in the Your Address section, click Add New Address. 
5. In the ADD NEW ADDRESS dialog box, enter the required information. 
6. Click SAVE ADDRESS. 
 
 
View products 
You can view the products and add them to your cart. For easy access to products, the shopping portal 
displays the products in the following categories: 
• 
Men 
• 
Women 
• 
Kids 
• 
Sports Accessories 
Note: 
The are other categories and this section is updated frequently to provide you the latest products 
offerings. 
To view products: 
1. Log in to the shopping portal. 
2. Click the menu icon 
. 
3. In the DISCOVER section, click the required category. For example, click WOMEN. 
4. Click on the required product card. For example, click FOOTWEAR. 
5. Click on the required product to view its details. 
6. Enter the required details such as size, color, and so on. 
7. Click ADD TO CART. 
8. Click GO TO CART. 
The product that your selected will be added to your shopping cart. 
Complete the order 
You can complete your purchase by completing the order. You will be required to provide your payment 
information and any other relevant details. 
To complete the order: 
1. On the shopping cart page, review the information on the CART SUMMARY section. 
2. Click PROCEED TO CHECKOUT. 
3. Select the Billing/Delivery address. 
4. To select the delivery options, click SELECT DELIVERY OPTIONS. 
5. Click SECURE PAYMENT. 
6. Enter the payment details. 
7. Click CONFIRM. 
Your order is completed.
