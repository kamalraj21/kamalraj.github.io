Getting Started

You can buy your favorite sporting gear and accessories from the online shopping portal. The portal features the latest products, discounts, rewards, and more.

This guide introduces you to the online shopping portal and its key features. It provides information for the most common tasks you will perform while shopping.

Logging In

You must log in to the shopping portal to access all available features. As a prerequisite, you must have an existing account. If you are a new customer, sign up first.

To log in:

  1. Go to https://www.decathlon.in.
  2. On the home page, click Sign In.
  3. In the LOGIN dialog box, enter your email or phone number.
  4. Click LOGIN VIA OTP.

You are now logged in to the shopping portal.

Purchasing Items

The shopping portal is designed to provide a hassle-free shopping experience. This section outlines the tasks needed to buy products and accessories.

Creating an Account

You require an account to log in and complete your order. Your account contains your details and allows you to view or manage your orders. If you already have an account, you can skip this step.

To create an account:

  1. On the home page, click Sign In.
  2. In the LOGIN dialog box, click the SIGNUP tab.
  3. Enter your email or phone number, and then click CONTINUE.
  4. Enter the 4-digit OTP sent to your device.
  5. Click VALIDATE OTP.

Updating Your Profile

After creating your account, update your profile to add your personal details, such as name, mobile number, and email address.

To update personal information:

  1. Log in to the shopping portal.
  2. On the home page, click Account > My profile.
  3. On the My Profile tab, under the Personal Information section, enter your details.
  4. Click Update.

To update your address:

  1. Log in to the shopping portal.
  2. On the home page, click Account > My profile.
  3. Click the Address tab, and then click Add New Address.
  4. In the ADD NEW ADDRESS dialog box, enter the required information.
  5. Click SAVE ADDRESS.

Viewing Products

For easy access, the shopping portal organizes products into several categories, including Men, Women, Kids, and Sports Accessories.

Note

Additional categories exist, and this section updates frequently to feature the latest products.

To view and select products:

  1. Log in to the shopping portal.
  2. Click the menu icon.
  3. Under the DISCOVER section, click the desired category. For example, click WOMEN.
  4. Click the desired product card. For example, click FOOTWEAR.
  5. Click a specific product to view its details.
  6. Select the required attributes, such as size and color.
  7. Click ADD TO CART.
  8. Click GO TO CART.

Completing Your Order

To finalize your purchase, review your order and provide payment information.

To complete the order:

  1. On the shopping cart page, review the information in the CART SUMMARY section.
  2. Click PROCEED TO CHECKOUT.
  3. Select the billing or delivery address.
  4. To select the delivery method, click SELECT DELIVERY OPTIONS.
  5. Click SECURE PAYMENT.
  6. Enter your payment details, and then click CONFIRM.

Your order is now complete.
